## Phenomenological Characteristics

### The "Oneirophrenic" Quality
Ibogaine produces dream-like, visionary states distinct from "classical" serotonergic psychedelics.

- [Naranjo 1969](../1969/Naranjo1969_Psychotherapeutic_Possibilities_Fantasy-Enhancing_Drugs.md) — **FOUNDATIONAL**: Claudio Naranjo's early work on ibogaine as "fantasy-enhancing." Compares ibogaine and harmaline as oneirophrenics.
- [La Torre 2025](../2025/LaTorre2025_Phenomenology_Thematic_Analysis.md) — **LARGEST PHENOMENOLOGY STUDY**: n=471 participants. Thematic analysis capturing emotional amplification, life review, perceptual alteration, visionary states, and ibogaine's perceived "agency." Beond clinical setting data.
- [Heink 2017](../2017/Heink2017_Phenomenology_Ibogaine_ASC.md) — **FIRST 5d-ASC APPLICATION TO IBOGAINE**: n=27 online survey using the 5-Dimensional Altered States of Consciousness scale. Ibogaine exceeded high-dose psilocybin (315 μg/kg) across all six ASC dimensions, with the largest discrepancy in Auditory Alterations (54.87 vs 11.53). Visual Restructuralisation correlated with perceived change (rho=.679); 92% reported craving reduction at 4 weeks. Critically, no correlation between ASC intensity and perceived effectiveness — suggesting pharmacological and experiential therapeutic mechanisms may be dissociable.
- [Ivan 2023](../2024/Ivan2024_Nonclassic_Psychedelic_Ibogaine_Disrupts_Cognitive_Maps.md) — "Nonclassic psychedelic" — disrupts cognitive maps differently from serotonergic psychedelics
- [Barsuglia 2020](../2020/Kohek2020_Qualitive_Study_Acute_Subjective_Effects_of_Ibogaine.md) — Acute subjective effects characterisation

### Transpersonal Dimensions
- [Dickinson 2023](../2023/Dickinson2023_Transpersonal_Intersubjectivity_in_Ibogaine_Experiences.md) — **TRANSPERSONAL INTERSUBJECTIVITY**: Three in-depth narrative case reports spanning clinical (Mexico), traditional Bwiti initiation (Gabon), and solo home use. Goes beyond phenomenological *content* categorisation to investigate the *relational* quality of ibogaine experiences — all three participants reported a distinct sense of transpersonal communication with ibogaine itself or entities encountered in the oneirogenic state. Connects this to waking-REM phenomena (sleep paralysis). Author (Ambio Life Sciences) brings decade of practitioner experience and Bwiti initiation. Published in *Anthropology of Consciousness*.
- [Özmat 2024](../2024/Ozmat2024_Subjective_Ibogaine_Experiences_Intersection_Social-Ecological_Dimensions.md) — Subjective experiences across intersecting social-ecological dimensions

### Cultural/Ritual Context
- [Rodger 2018](../2018/Rodger2018_Healing_Potential_Ibogaine.md) — Comparative phenomenological analysis integrating Bwiti context
- [Goutarel, Gollnhofer & Sillans 1993](../1993/Goutarel1993_Ibogaine_Pharmacodynamics_Therapeutic_Applications.md) — **MITSOGHO BWITI VISIONARY TAXONOMY**: CNRS ethnographic fieldwork documenting four distinct stages of iboga-induced visionary experience during Mitsogho initiation: (1) incoherent imagery, (2) threatening animal apparitions, (3) personally meaningful mythic visions, (4) "normative visions" — encounter with spiritual entities and the collective tribal cosmology. A distinct ethnographic tradition from [Fernandez 2001](../2001/Fernandez2001_Returning_to_the_Path_Bwiti_Ritual_Context.md)'s Fang Bwiti fieldwork. Also draws structural parallels between these Bwiti normative visions, Naranjo's psychoanalytic oneiric stages, and near-death experiences. See [WHITE_Historical_Hub](WHITE_Historical_Hub.md) for full entry.
- [Rodríguez-Cano 2022](../2022/Rodriguez-Cano2022_Underground_Ibogaine_Use_for_SUD_Tx_Qualitative_Analysis_Subjective_Experiences.md) — **UNDERGROUND SETTINGS**: ICEERS-led grounded theory analysis (n=13) of ibogaine use in non-clinical contexts — homes, motels, clinics without official recognition. International sample (7 countries). Three core psychological categories emerged: autobiographical/interpersonal processing, abreactive resolution, and transpersonal experiences. Uniquely documents the ecological reality of unregulated ibogaine use: 11/13 motivated by SUD, all reported mixed pleasant/unpleasant experience, and participants universally emphasised that ibogaine alone does not guarantee recovery — integration, motivation, and aftercare are decisive.
- [INTASH 1990](../Primary_Sources/INTASH1990_Ibogaine_Experience_Reflections.md) — **EARLIEST EUROPEAN PHENOMENOLOGICAL PRIMARY SOURCE**: Anonymous first-person account of a 1989 Amsterdam ibogaine treatment — the earliest documented phenomenological narrative from European ibogaine use in the vault. Documents a full oneiric arc: geometric onset, stroboscopic childhood memory revisitation (at the visual height of a four-year-old), African drum-triggered visionary sequences, spontaneous rebirthing experience, and withdrawal resolution without conventional detoxification. The author and her boyfriend were the first people treated with ibogaine in the Netherlands. This account directly catalysed the founding of INTASH (International Addict Self-Help) — the movement that [Frenken 2001](../2001/Frenken2001_RootsUp_INTASH_SelfHelp.md) later documented academically. A critical early insight: relapse after ~1 year due to inadequate aftercare anticipated decades of research on integration necessity. Compare [MyEboga 2008](../Other/MyEboga2008_Experience_Reports.md), the later community compilation in the same phenomenological documentation tradition this account helped inaugurate.
- [MyEboga Experiences (~2008)](../Other/MyEboga2008_Experience_Reports.md) — **LARGEST FIRST-PERSON COMPILATION**: 12 detailed narrative accounts (6 chemical dependence, 6 non-dependence) from myeboga.com. Corroborates three-phase model from inside; documents fear as therapeutic gateway, perceived guiding intelligence, somatic processing, life review. Richest primary-source phenomenological archive in the vault.
- [MYEBOGA 2020](../Primary_Sources/2020_MYEBOGA_Clare_Wilkins_Interview.md) — Clare Wilkins podcast interview: 800+ treatments, community perspectives

## Psychological & Therapeutic Mechanisms

### Trauma Processing
- [Lotsof 1996](../1996/Lotsof1996_Trauma_Abreaction.md) — **FOUNDATIONAL**: Howard Lotsof's framework for trauma and abreaction in ibogaine therapy
- [Bastiaans 2004](../2004/Bastiaans2004_Life_After_Ibogaine.md) — "Life After Ibogaine": qualitative outcomes research
- [Camlin 2018](../2018/Camlin2018_Phenomenological_Ibogaine_Opioid_Use.md) — **PHENOMENOLOGICAL INVESTIGATION (CROSSROADS)**: Constant comparative method (n=10 OUD, Barsuglia/Polanco clinic). 100% reported withdrawal attenuation, craving elimination, and increased self-acceptance; 90% had traumatic histories driving substance use. Novel finding: 70% described subjective localised brain activation — "zapping" or "cleaning" of neurons — a phenomenon undocumented in prior literature. 80% experienced mixed dark/scary and joyful/peaceful imagery, consistent with exposure-based trauma processing. Authors propose integration with CBT, dream analysis, and logotherapy.
- [Brown & Noller 2019](../2019/Brown2019_Ibogaine_Subjective_Experience_OUD.md) — **SCQ + THEMATIC ANALYSIS (MEXICO/NZ)**: Two-site observational study (n=44; Mexico n=30, New Zealand n=14). 43% achieved "complete mystical experience" on the States of Consciousness Questionnaire. Thematic analysis revealed a hierarchical process: anxiety → life review → confrontation → remorse → empathy → letting go → transformation (the most prominent theme, 32 utterances). Critically, no significant correlation between SCQ scores and treatment outcomes at 1, 6, or 12 months — consistent with Heink 2017's finding that ASC intensity and clinical effectiveness may be dissociable. Proposes ibogaine's oneiric effects as a discrete therapeutic element, not mere epiphenomenon. Co-authored with Geoff Noller.
- [Olash 2026](../2026/Olash2026_Subjective_Experience_Magnesium_Ibogaine.md) — **MISTIC PHENOMENOLOGY** (npj Mental Health Research): Qualitative companion to Cherian 2024. n=30 SOF veterans describe four domains: dialogic trauma reappraisal, altered-self/mystical connection, emotional resolution, embodied brain healing. Frames ibogaine as "accelerated auto-psychotherapy" compressing CBT, exposure, and humanistic processes into single session. *Dedicated to Nolan Williams (d. 2025).*
- [Brown R 2025](../2025/Brown2025_Mystical_Experience_PTSD_Veterans.md) — **MISTIC: MYSTICAL EXPERIENCE PREDICTS PTSD OUTCOMES** (Journal of Affective Disorders): Quantitative companion to Olash 2026. n=30 SOF veterans with TBI. MEQ30 intensity predicted larger PTSD reductions both immediately (B=-5.89, p<.001) and at 1 month (B=-4.45, p=.007). Greater mystical experience also associated with sustained decreases in peak alpha frequency on EEG — a neurophysiological marker previously linked to ibogaine's clinical effects. Unlike Heink 2017 and Brown 2019's null findings with 5d-ASC/SCQ, the MEQ30 captured a significant experience-outcome relationship, suggesting instrument sensitivity matters for ibogaine's atypical phenomenology.

### Narrative Self & Identity Transformation
- [Underwood 2025](../2025/Underwood2025_PhD_Experiences_Part_of_Journey.md) — **PhD THESIS: FIRST PCI APPLICATION TO IBOGAINE**: Canterbury Christ Church doctoral research comprising PCI consciousness mapping (n=21), narrative self questionnaire (n=38) + IPA interviews (n=10), and 32-month longitudinal case study. Visual imagery correlates with self-awareness. 97% report narrative self transformation across 11 dimensions (agency, autobiographical reasoning, meaning-making, etc.). Proposes integration protocol framework positioning psychedelic experiences as central to recovery.

### Integration & Aftercare
- [Kroupa & Wells 2005](../2005/Kroupa2005_Boosters_Maintenance.md) — The "noribogaine window" concept for integration

### Consumer Perspectives & Online Communities
- [Barber et al. 2020](../2020/Barber2020_Consumer_Views_Online_Fora.md) — **FIRST SYSTEMATIC STUDY OF ONLINE COMMUNITIES**: Qualitative thematic analysis of Reddit and Drugs Forum (n=101 participants). Documents how fora serve as DIY research hubs; reveals tension between pharmacological "brain reset" and visionary experience frameworks; shows cost barriers driving home administration despite safety preference for clinics. Important harm reduction implications.

## Quantitative Assessment

### Scale Development
- [Espejito 2025](../2025/Espejito2025_Ibogaine_Experience_Scale_Psychometrics_Subjective.md) — **IES VALIDATION**: 70-item Ibogaine Experience Scale capturing oneiric/dream-like subjective effects. ICEERS-led psychometric development.
- [Underwood 2025](../2025/Underwood2025_PhD_Experiences_Part_of_Journey.md) — **FIRST PCI APPLICATION**: Phenomenology of Consciousness Inventory applied to ibogaine (n=21). Visual imagery strongly correlates with self-awareness (r=.61). Establishes baseline for future consciousness mapping research.
- [Heink 2017](../2017/Heink2017_Phenomenology_Ibogaine_ASC.md) — **FIRST 5d-ASC APPLICATION**: Establishes ibogaine's ASC profile as quantitatively more intense than high-dose psilocybin across all six dimensions. Provides benchmark data for future comparative psychometric research.
- [Brown & Noller 2019](../2019/Brown2019_Ibogaine_Subjective_Experience_OUD.md) — **SCQ APPLICATION**: States of Consciousness Questionnaire (Griffiths et al. 2006) applied to ibogaine (n=42). 43% achieved complete mystical experience. Both this study and Heink 2017 found no ASC-outcome correlation — prompting the question of whether instruments designed for classical psychedelics capture ibogaine's therapeutic phenomenology.
- [Brown R 2025](../2025/Brown2025_Mystical_Experience_PTSD_Veterans.md) — **MEQ30 APPLICATION**: Revised Mystical Experiences Questionnaire (n=30 veterans). Unlike 5d-ASC and SCQ, the MEQ30 captured a significant experience-outcome relationship for PTSD, with EEG correlates. Suggests the MEQ30's focus on mystical/spiritual dimensions may better index ibogaine's therapeutically relevant experiential features.

## Adverse Psychological Effects

Not all experiences are positive — psychiatric risks require documentation.

- [Ibogaine & Mania 2015](../2015/Marta2015_Ibogaine_Mania_Case_Reports.md) — Three case reports of manic episodes following ibogaine
- [Breuer 2015](../2015/Breuer2015_Herbal_Seizures_Case_Report.md) — "Herbal seizures" — atypical symptoms including seizure (see also [RED_Cardiac_Safety_Hub](RED_Cardiac_Safety_Hub.md))
- [Knuijver 2018](../2018/Knuijver2018_HPPD_Ibogaine_Case_Report.md) — **FIRST REPORTED IBOGAINE HPPD** (BLUE primary): A 31-year-old opioid-dependent male re-experienced oneirogenic imagery 48 hours post-dose — his mother abandoning him as a foundling, discovering a deceased friend in a bathtub after a heroin overdose he had supplied. The re-experiencing triggered intense rejection, shame, and guilt, persisting ~5 weeks and meeting DSM-5 HPPD criteria. A single EMDR session targeting the distressing imagery produced substantial relief — images faded over 14 days — but craving persisted unchanged and the patient relapsed to heroin, demonstrating a clean dissociation between the phenomenological disturbance and the anti-addictive mechanism. The emotional specificity of the HPPD content (abandonment, survivor guilt) suggests unresolved traumatic material surfacing during the oneirogenic phase can become substrate for persisting re-experiencing. From the same EUDRACT trial as [Knuijver 2021](../2021/Knuijver2021_Safety_Opioid_Detox.md).

## Phenomenological Distinctions

| Feature | Ibogaine | Classical Psychedelics |
|---------|----------|----------------------|
| Primary quality | Oneiric (dream-like) | Perceptual/mystical |
| Duration | 24-36 hours | 4-8 hours |
| Visual content | Biographical, ancestors | Geometric, abstract |
| Body load | Heavy (ataxia, nausea) | Variable |
| Insight type | Life review, trauma | Ego dissolution |
