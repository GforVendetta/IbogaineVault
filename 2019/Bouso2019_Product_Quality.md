# CRITICAL SAFETY ALERT: Product Quality Crisis

## An Analytical Study of Iboga Alkaloids in Products from Treatment Providers
**Bouso et al., 2019**

Archives of Clinical Psychiatry 46(2):51-54
DOI: 10.1590/0101-60830000000231

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**This study reveals a CATASTROPHIC product quality crisis in the ibogaine market that poses extreme danger to patients.**

### Critical Findings:
- **n=16 products analyzed** from 5 vendors across 9 countries (2006-2013)
- **ONE SAMPLE CONTAINED NO IBOGAINE** (sold as iboga product)
- **Extreme purity variation**: 0-73.4% ibogaine (claimed vs actual)
- **Unknown contaminants** found in almost all samples
- **Dose calculation impossible** without analysis

---

## PRODUCTS ANALYZED

### Sample Types (n=16):
1. **Iboga root bark** (n=6)
2. **Total Alkaloids (TA)** (n=5)
3. **Purified Total Alkaloids (PTA HCl)** (n=1)
4. **Ibogaine HCl** (n=3)
5. **Voacanga africana bark** (n=1)

### Geographic Distribution:
Australia, Netherlands, New Zealand, Canada, Mexico, Germany, South Africa, Spain, Hawaii USA

### Purchase Timeline:
2006-2013 (samples submitted 2013 study)

---

## QUANTITATIVE RESULTS - CATASTROPHIC VARIABILITY

### Root Bark Samples (n=6):
| Range | Finding |
|-------|---------|
| **0.6% - 11.2%** ibogaine | **18x variation** |
| Expected: 2-7% | **Actual: 0.6-11.2%** |
| **ONE SAMPLE: 0% ibogaine** | **Completely misrepresented** |

**Analysis:** One "root bark" sample contained **ZERO iboga alkaloids** - confirmed fraud or misidentification

### Total Alkaloids/TA Extract (n=5):
| Range | Finding |
|-------|---------|
| **8.2% - 32.9%** ibogaine | **4x variation** |
| Expected: 35-40% | **Actual: 8.2-32.9%** |
| Largest discrepancy: 27% | **Sold as 35%, actually 8.2%** |

### "Purified" Ibogaine HCl (n=3):
| Range | Finding |
|-------|---------|
| **61.5% - 73.4%** | Expected >90% purity |
| Contained ibogamine | 2.1% - 8.7% contamination |
| Contained ibogaline | Up to 7.2% |

**Note:** Some "ibogaine HCl" samples were actually PTA based on alkaloid profiles

### Voacanga africana (n=1):
- **Expected:** 5-10% voacangine
- **Actual:** 2.1% voacangine
- Also contained: 0.6% ibogaine (unexpected)

## CONTAMINATION & UNKNOWN SUBSTANCES

### Confirmed Issues:
1. **Unknown substances** detected in multiple samples (unidentified by GC/MS)
2. **Cross-contamination** between alkaloid types
3. **Mislabeled products** (wrong plant species)
4. **Variable alkaloid profiles** suggesting different processing/sources

### Historical Context - Reserpine Contamination:
- **Slovenia 2011 fatality:** Reserpine (hypotensive drug) found in victim's blood
- **Slovenia 2012:** Reserpine detected in ibogaine samples
- **Mechanism:** Reserpine + ibogaine = synergistic hypotension + CYP2D6 competition
- **Risk:** Increased blood ibogaine levels → overdose

---

## CLINICAL SAFETY IMPLICATIONS

### Dose Calculation Impossible Without Analysis:

**Example Scenario:**
- Provider expects: Root bark with 5% ibogaine
- Plans dose: 20g root bark = 1,000mg ibogaine
- **Actual range: 0.6% - 11.2%**
  - Worst case (11.2%): 20g = 2,240mg ibogaine (**2.2x overdose**)
  - Best case (0.6%): 20g = 120mg ibogaine (**underdose, no effect**)

### Overdose Risk Factors:
1. **Variability:** 18x difference in root bark, 4x in TA
2. **Mislabeling:** Claimed vs actual content discrepancies
3. **Unknown contaminants:** Potential drug interactions
4. **No quality control:** Online vendors, no regulation

### Cardiovascular Danger:
- Ibogaine prolongs QT interval (dose-dependent)
- Overdose = extreme QT prolongation = torsades de pointes = death
- **Unknown contaminants** (like reserpine) potentiate cardiac risk
- Correct dosing **CRITICAL** for cardiac safety

## REGULATORY & MARKET CONTEXT

### Legal Status (2019):
- **New Zealand, Australia, South Africa:** Can prescribe for addiction
- **Most countries:** Unregulated or prohibited
- **Gabon:** Unlicensed export forbidden (primary source)
- **Result:** Black/grey market = no quality control

### Market Growth (2003-2008):
- **2003:** ~857 people used ibogaine
- **2008:** ~3,414 people (**4x increase**)
- **68%** used for addiction treatment
- **Rising fatalities:** 22 deaths by 2015

### Clinic Proliferation:
- Many operate outside medical settings (hotel rooms, retreat centers)
- Providers buy from web-based suppliers
- **No analysis of product before administration**

## METHODOLOGY

### Sample Collection:
- Advertisement through ICEERS Foundation (July 2013)
- Voluntary submission from providers, vendors, buyers
- Information requested:
  - Material type
  - Source/vendor
  - Purchase date
  - Expected concentration
  - Adverse effects observed

### Analysis:
- **Performed by:** Energy Control (Spanish NGO) + IMIM Barcelona
- **Method:** GC/MS (Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry)
- **Equipment:** Agilent 7890B GC + 5977A MS
- **Standards:** Phytostan Inc. (ibogaine, voacangine), REFORM Italia (ibogamine)
- **Validation:** High-purity standards from Martin Kuehne lab (University of Vermont)

---

## HISTORICAL FATALITIES & PRODUCT QUALITY LINK

### Confirmed Cases:
1. **Slovenia 2011:** Death after ibogaine ingestion
   - **Finding:** Reserpine in victim's blood
   - **Source unknown:** Sample not analyzed (may have been in ibogaine OR separate supplement)
   
2. **France 2013:** Death from "iboga bark"
   - **Finding:** Product was actually **Rauvolfia powder** (wrong plant)
   - **Consequence:** Zero ibogaine, potentially toxic Rauvolfia alkaloids

### Common Forensic Findings:
- Polypharmacy (multiple drugs present)
- Unknown ibogaine concentrations
- No product analysis conducted
- Uncontrolled settings (no medical supervision)

---

## RECOMMENDATIONS

### For Treatment Providers:
1. **MANDATORY:** Analyze ALL products before use (GC/MS minimum)
2. **Never trust vendor claims** without independent verification
3. Calculate doses based on **measured** concentrations only
4. Source from **pharmaceutical-grade suppliers only**
5. Maintain chain of custody documentation

### For Patients/Users:
1. **Demand certificate of analysis** from provider
2. Verify analysis is from **independent lab** (not vendor)
3. **Refuse treatment** without documented product analysis
4. Understand: Root bark = extreme variability = extreme danger without testing

### For Harm Reduction:
1. **Free drug testing services** for iboga products
2. Public database of analyzed samples (similar to EcstasyData)
3. Education on dose calculation errors
4. Warning system for contaminated batches

### For Regulators:
1. Pharmaceutical-grade ibogaine production standards
2. Mandatory testing requirements
3. Track-and-trace systems for legal markets
4. Enforcement against fraudulent products

---

## CONCLUSIONS

**This study documents a public health emergency:**

1. **Product fraud:** One sample contained NO ibogaine (sold as iboga)
2. **Extreme variability:** Up to 18x difference in same product category
3. **Systematic mislabeling:** Vendor claims unreliable
4. **Unknown contaminants:** Present in most samples
5. **Dose calculation impossible:** Without independent analysis

**The combination of:**
- Cardiac toxicity (QT prolongation)
- Unknown/variable dosing
- Potential contaminants
- Uncontrolled settings

**= FATAL RISK**

### Post-2013 Developments:
- Increased manufacture of high-purity ibogaine HCl from Voacanga
- Offers alternative to variable root bark products
- Still requires independent verification
- Pharmaceutical-grade synthesis now available

## CRITICAL TAKE-HOME MESSAGE

**NEVER ADMINISTER IBOGAINE WITHOUT:**
1. Independent GC/MS analysis of specific batch
2. Documented ibogaine concentration
3. Screening for contaminants
4. Pharmaceutical-grade product preferred
5. Medical supervision with cardiac monitoring

**ROOT BARK = RUSSIAN ROULETTE** without analysis

**The variability documented in this study makes accurate dosing impossible without testing, and accurate dosing is the difference between life and death given ibogaine's cardiotoxic properties.**
