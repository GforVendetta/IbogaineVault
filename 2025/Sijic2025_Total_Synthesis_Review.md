# Totalna Sinteza Ibogaina (Total Synthesis of Ibogaine)

**Citation:** Šijić, B. (2025). Totalna Sinteza Ibogaina [Završni rad, Undergraduate Thesis]. Sveučilište u Zagrebu, Prirodoslovno-matematički fakultet, Kemijski odsjek. Mentor: izv. prof. dr. sc. Đani Škalamera.

## Abstract

This Croatian undergraduate thesis provides a comprehensive literature review of ibogaine total synthesis methodologies spanning nearly 60 years of synthetic chemistry (1966-2025). The work surveys six major synthetic approaches, documenting the evolution from early low-yielding routes to modern gram-scale enantioselective methods.

## Key Findings

- **Büchi et al. (1966):** First total synthesis — 15 steps, 0.2% overall yield
- **Jana & Sinha (2012):** Intramolecular reductive Heck reaction — 9 steps, 9.4% yield
- **Zhao et al. (2016):** Biomimetic synthesis via intramolecular aldol condensation — 12 steps, 4.6% yield
- **Iyer et al. (2025):** Three syntheses achieving modern efficiency:
  - (±)-Epiibogaine: 8 steps, 8.7% yield
  - (±)-Ibogaine gram-scale: 7 steps, 10-14% yield
  - (+)-Ibogaine asymmetric: 11 steps, 9.3% yield

## Background Content

The thesis includes introductory material on:
- Total synthesis principles (Wöhler, Kolbe, Fischer, Woodward, Corey contributions)
- Iboga alkaloid structural features
- Historical discovery (Dybovsky & Landrin 1901; Haller & Heckel isolation)
- Physical/chemical properties (C₂₀H₂₆N₂O, Mr=310.44, mp 153°C)
- Clinical applications in addiction treatment
- Metabolic pathway (CYP2D6-mediated O-demethylation to noribogaine, glucuronidation, excretion)

## Clinical Implications

This synthesis review has limited direct clinical relevance but documents the increasing accessibility of ibogaine through improved synthetic routes. The 2025 Iyer gram-scale synthesis (7 steps, 10-14% yield) represents a practical laboratory-scale source independent of plant extraction.

## Limitations

- Literature review only — no original experimental work
- Written in Croatian (may limit accessibility)
- Undergraduate thesis level
